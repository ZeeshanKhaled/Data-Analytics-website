Research and Insights

Our own research has concluded that within the last 10 years peoples presentations on data have become more vibrant and more colourful. This is not just a coincidence as it has been scientifically proven that peoples attention spans have lowered; this is due to the increase in short term media which has meant presenters need to ensure their presentations are more eye catching, leading to people actually paying attention.
